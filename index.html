Our Growth Story

Semester 1: Foundation

3 Executive Members | 20 Registered Members

Established UNC NSBE with a vision to create a home for black engineers and build community.

Semester 2: Acceleration

8 Executive Members | 40 Registered Members

70% retention rate. Doubled membership and expanded programming. Sponsored 3 full conference attendees and 5 partial.

Semester 3: Excellence

14 Executive Members | 120 Registered Members

100% retention rate. Became the heart of UNC Engineering. Trusted to start the engineering program at UNC.
